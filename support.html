Does Structura read or store chat messages?

No. The bot only reads slash commands and does not store messages or logs.

Can I delete channels after Structura creates them?

Yes. The bot will not recreate deleted channels unless you run a setup or load a template.

How do I save a server layout?

Use /template-save to store the entire channel + role structure.
